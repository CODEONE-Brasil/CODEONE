Being a CODEONE affiliate station is being part of a vibrant, innovative and constantly expanding ecosystem, with access to exclusive content, high-quality programming and technical and creative support to strengthen your local presence - with a national identity.

What is a CODEONE Affiliate Station?

A CODEONE affiliate station maintains its local autonomy, but becomes part of our content distribution network, being able to:

  • Reproduce parts of CODEONE's official programming (partially or fully);
  • Have access to ready-to-broadcast playlists, programs, jingles and thematic blocks;
  • Participate in joint campaigns, events and promotional actions;
  • Receive editorial, artistic and technical support;
  • Integrate your own content into the network, as long as approved by CODEMEDIA's curation.

For whom?

Ideal for:

  • Regional web radios that want to strengthen their programming;
  • FM or AM stations seeking digitization and sound updating;
  • Local projects that want to maintain their identity, but with an "upgrade" in quality, standard and support.

Benefits of being a CODEONE Affiliate:

  • High-quality musical and editorial content
  • Access to CODEONE identity (jingles, visuals, brand)

  • Participation in network projects and launches
  • Partnership with a brand that values innovation and respect for each listener's time

CODEPARTY

The party never stops at CODEONE! Have you ever imagined honoring someone special with a live radio show, fully personalized with the songs that marked that person's history and voice messages recorded by friends and family?

With CODEPARTY, this is possible.

What is CODEPARTY?

An exclusive radio show, broadcast live on the internet, created to celebrate people and unforgettable moments. Ideal for birthdays, weddings, promotions, farewells, reunions and commemorative dates in general.

You choose:

  • 🎶 The most striking songs
  • 🎤 Voice messages from loved ones
  • 🗓️ The day and time of transmission

The narration is done by a professional announcer with experience in Brazil's biggest radio stations, such as Rede Transamérica and Metropolitana FM.

How does it work?

  1. Personalized scheduling
    You contact us and we schedule a conversation to understand your idea, the occasion and the best day/time for transmission.
  2. Content definition
    You select the songs and send voice messages (via WhatsApp) from friends and family. We take care of the assembly and emotion.
  3. Live broadcast
    On the scheduled day and time, the show airs on CODEONE radio (www.codeonebrasil.com.br) and can be heard from anywhere in the world.
  4. Recorded show as a souvenir
    After transmission, you receive the complete show in MP3 format to keep, share or relive whenever you want.

For whom is CODEPARTY?

  • ❤️ Couples in love (Valentine's Day, dating or marriage anniversaries)
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Parents on commemorative dates
  • 🎉 Friends on birthdays, graduations or farewells
  • 📚 Unforgettable reunions (ex: school groups)
  • 🏢 Companies that want to honor employees or celebrate achievements
